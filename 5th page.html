Huskies

Personality

Huskies are intelligent and independant, but
at the same time, they are also affectionate
and loyal to their owners.

Size and weight

Males are 21-23.5 inches tall and 45-60
pounds. Females are 20-22 inches tall and
35-50 pounds.

Food

Besides commercial pet food, huskies can also eat chicken,
beef, lamb and fruit apart from grapes.

Grooming

Huskies need very little grooming compared
to other breeds, just brushing from time to
time to get rid of dead undercoat and hair.

Lifespan

Huskies reach maturity when they are 1 year
old. They live for about 12-15 years.
