Border collies

Personality

Border collies are smart, athletic dog
breeds who love working. They need a
large space and an owner who is dedicated
and has time for them.

Size and weight

Males stand 19-22 inches tall and weigh 35-40 pounds.
Females stand 18-21 inches and weigh 30-40 pounds.

Food

Border collies can eat a raw diet with raw meat, raw
vegetables, fish and eggs. However, they should not
be fed grains as many border collies cannot digest
them properly.

Grooming

During most of the year, border collies shed
a moderate amount. Brushing it 2-3 times
every week will suffice.

Lifespan

Border collies live 12-15 years on average.
They reach adulthood at about 15 months
old.
