Golden retrievers

Personality

Golden retrievers are even-tempered,
intelligent and affectionate. They are
gentle with children and can get along
with other pets.

Size and weight

Males are 23-24 inches tall and weigh 65-70 pounds.
Females are 21.5-22.5 inches tall and weigh 55-60 pounds.

Food

Golden retrievers eat a mixture of dry kibble, meat,
vegetables and canned food. They drink 0.5-1 oz of water
per day. It is advised to change their water at least 3
times a day.

Grooming

Golden retrievers require frequent
grooming and attention to maintain a happy
and healthy lifestyle.

Lifespan

Golden retrievers become adults at 18
months and typically live 10-12 years.
