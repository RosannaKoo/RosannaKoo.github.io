King Charles spaniels

Personality

King Charles spaniels come in a
range of personalties, but most
of them are quiet and docile. They
are also quite playful.

Size and weight

King Charles spaniels are usually
12-13 inches tall, and 13-18 pounds
when fully grown.

Food

Too high calorie foods for these dogs cause
them to gain weight rapidly. Small amounts of
boiled liver or chicken is suitable for them
instead.

Grooming

brushing with a middle-sized bristle
brush to spread the dogâ€™s natural
oils over its coat.

Lifespan

King Charles spaniels mature when
they are about 12 months old and
live for 9-14 years.
